DMCA Overview

We take intellectual property rights seriously and comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). If you believe that content linked from our site infringes your copyright, please follow the procedure outlined below.

Please Note: TBCPL is a directory service that provides links to third-party sites. We do not host, store, or control any content. All content is hosted by independent third-party websites. We have no control over the content on these third-party sites.

How We Handle DMCA Requests

1

Review

We review all valid DMCA requests submitted via email

2

Verify

We verify that all required information is included

3

Action

We remove links to infringing content when appropriate

4

Notify

We notify you of the action taken on your request

DMCA Request Requirements

Description of Copyrighted Work

A description of the copyrighted work that you claim is being infringed

Location of Infringing Material

The URL(s) or location of the material you claim is infringing, with enough detail for us to locate it

Your Contact Information

Your name, title (if acting as an agent), address, telephone number, and email address

Good Faith Statement

"I have a good faith belief that the use of the copyrighted material I am complaining of is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law (e.g., as a fair use)."

Accuracy Statement

"The information in this notice is accurate and, under penalty of perjury, I am the owner, or authorized to act on behalf of the owner, of the copyright or of an exclusive right that is allegedly infringed."

Legal Accountability Statement

"I understand that I am subject to legal action upon submitting a DMCA request without solid proof."

Signature

An electronic or physical signature of the owner of the copyright or a person authorized to act on the owner's behalf
