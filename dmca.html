DMCA Overview

We take intellectual property rights seriously and comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). If you believe that content linked from our site infringes your copyright, please follow the procedure outlined below.


Please Note: TBCPL is a directory service that provides links to third-party sites. We do not host, store, or control any content. All content is hosted by independent third-party websites.

Our Process

1

Review

We review all valid requests submitted via email.

2

Verify

We verify all required information is included.

3

Action

We remove links to infringing content promptly.

Request Requirements

Description of the copyrighted work.

Exact URL(s) of the infringing material.

Your full contact information.

A statement of "Good Faith" belief.

A signature (physical or electronic).
